📡 Globalstar Seeks IN-SPACe Approval to Launch iPhone Satellite Services in India

This signals India’s openness to satcom innovation and could catalyze new partnerships and public-private integration in the space-tech domain.

🛰️ Intelsat & Northrop Grumman Achieve Historic Life-Extension

In a first-of-its-kind operation, MEV-1 extended the life of Intelsat 901 by five years. This marks a turning point in satellite lifecycle and sustainability.

The success promises flexible, long-term satellite missions and highlights innovation in geostationary orbit operations.
